What is Tableau?

Tableau is a powerful and widely-used data visualization tool that helps individuals and organizations transform raw data into interactive, insightful, and easy-to-understand visual representations. Known for its user-friendly drag-and-drop interface, Tableau allows users‚Äîeven those without any coding background‚Äîto connect with various data sources such as Excel, SQL databases, cloud services, and more. Users can create a wide range of visualizations like bar charts, line graphs, pie charts, maps, and dashboards to explore data trends, patterns, and insights. Tableau supports real-time data analysis, enabling professionals to make data-driven decisions quickly and efficiently. It provides powerful features like calculated fields, filters, parameters, table calculations, and interactive dashboards that allow users to drill down into data and find deeper insights. With Tableau Public and Tableau Server/Online, users can also publish and share their reports securely with teams or the public. In this project, Tableau was used to visualize complex datasets, making it easier to identify trends, outliers, and actionable insights. Whether for business intelligence, academic analysis, or project reporting, Tableau plays a vital role in bridging the gap between data and decision-making, making it an essential tool in the modern data-driven world.
